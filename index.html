About Me

Hello! I’m Kawindi and this is my website. Here you can find a collection of all the projects that I’ve worked on, or are currently in the works - both work related and personal. I graduated with my bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering from the University of British Columbia (UBC) in May 2017. Since then, I’ve been working for MDA, a space technology company, as a software developer. My current work contributes to the development and deployment of ground systems for Earth observation satellites.

Because I focused my degree on electrical engineering while gaining industry experience in software development, I have a good balance of both hardware and software knowledge. It has also engrained in me the mathematical rigour that is sometimes necessary to discover a less obvious but more elegant solution. I love tackling challenging problems and try to approach problem solving from an organized and methodical perspective. I feel that it has allowed me to achieve a deeper level of understanding of the task and, ultimately, the end goal. Thanks for stopping by. If you would like, feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn or send me an email.
